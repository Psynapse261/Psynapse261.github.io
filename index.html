My Motivation

I once read a paragraph on the internet which sums up the world of computer science with a fantasy novel twist. This little paragraph motivates me and shows to me that programming can do wonderful things:

There are these rocks found on the shores of the earth, which are purified to the utmost degree, and then transformed into a uniform sheet. Archai sigils and symbols are inscribed on this sheet, all interconnected to form a geometric painting. When imbued with electricity, these sheets start to speak in a language unbeknownst to anyone, except for a few trained wizards. The wizards harness the power of these stones and make it possible to store and share knowledge, run a global business, produce new forms of entertainment, and talk to a loved one on the other side of the world.
